The life of Charles Spurgeon, the "Prince of Preachers"

Portrait of Spurgeon by Alexander Melville (1885)

A few facts about our guy, Chuck.

> Charles was born on 19 June 1834 in Essex, England.

> He was a Calvinist and held a Reformed Baptist theology, but when asked about his creed, he famously replied: "It is Jesus Christ".

> He is often called the "Prince of Preachers", but in addition to preaching, he also wrote hymns and published a hymnal called Our Own Hymn Book, and he was probably the creator of the Wordless Book, an evangelism booklet containing no words.

"The Lord gets his best soldiers out of the highlands of affliction."
